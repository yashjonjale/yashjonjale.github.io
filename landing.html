More About Me

Few words for myself

Unlike many of my counterparts in the CSE department, I was not ambitious about my career. I was not involved in any of the scholarly stuff in the beginning. I was just a carefree person living a life. It was at the beginning of high school that I became more serious and thought about what I wanted to actually do in my life. This is when I started preparing for the very competitive JEE Advanced Examination. After two years of intense preparation involving 14 hours of time commitment every day, going through plenty of rough patches, and experimenting with various ways of learning, I cracked the examination. I received admission at the best institute in India, in the department of my choice. Since then, I began my computer science journey with a liberal attitude, exploring different subjects. It was around this time that I started losing my vision, what I had planned long ago. I started exploring a lot of things that were entirely different from my field. I scored an excellent grade of 9.79 at the end of the first year, fueling my overconfidence. Never before, in recent times, had I been so lost and lacking motivation. This continued till the third semester, and there came the massive and much-needed fall. My performance in that semester fell down to as little as 8. This is when I again sat down alone, thinking about what was wrong, fixing my vision and finding the right direction. And there I did that, and I am back again with a resolved and better vision, much stronger mentally and emotionally chasing my dreams!
